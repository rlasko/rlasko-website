The Project
Formal reports are often created to pitch an idea for a product, service, or system. Because readers initially scan pages rather than doing a deep read, it is important that headings and images capture and quickly communicate the gist of a project or investigation.
Create a harmonious design for a 16-page report that speaks simultaneously to the needs of both the clients and audiences. In particular, focus on gaining basic skills in design thinking and design method.
Individual Assignment | 3.5 Weeks | Fall 2016
Client: Walmart
Audiences: Customers and Shareholders
